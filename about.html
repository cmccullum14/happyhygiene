About Happy Hygiene

At Happy Hygiene, we believe that happiness starts with wellness and hygiene. There are numerous people in the world who aren't able to recieve proper hygiene materials to lead healthy lives. That's where Happy Hygiene can help!

Whether you are a college student, a full-time worker, or someone without transportation, Happy Hygiene is here to provide proper hygiene kits to improve your health and wellness. By subscribing with Happy Hygiene, you're able to build your own box with organic, company-brand hygiene products that will be shipped to your home!

At Happy Hygiene, we believe in being simple and convienient. Build your box today!


Our Mission

Happy Hygiene believes in providing hygiene for all people. That's why with every purchase, proceeds go into creating hygiene kits for those who are less fortunate.

Happy Hygiene works with local homeless shelters to provide personal hygiene kits to those who struggle with poverty and homelessness. It's our mission to support those who also deserve to have clean hygienic practices, and to be happy!
